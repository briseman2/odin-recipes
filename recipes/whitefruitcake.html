Description

A classic recipe for dessert, snacktime, and even breakfast if you're in the mood for something sweet in the morning!

For best results, serve with fresh fruit and/or yogurt!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups golden raisins
  • 1 ½ cups candied pineapple chunks
  • 1 ½ cups candied cherries
  • 1 cup dried currants
  • 2 ounces candied orange peel
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • 2 cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 8 large eggs, separated
  • 4 cups pecans, chopped
